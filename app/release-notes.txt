Issue #3 - Custom recurrence options.

New "Custom..." option in the recurrence picker:

- Every N hours (no sub-options)
- Every N days
- Every N weeks (with day-of-week chip selector)
- Every N months:
    - Same date each month (e.g. the 15th), or
    - Day of week of month (e.g. "2nd Tuesday")

Existing Daily / Weekly / Monthly quick-picks are unchanged.
