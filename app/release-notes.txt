v1.1 — Scheduled task fixes + diagnostics

- Fixed: scheduled tasks were not emitting into the task list. Root cause was a missing
  alarm permission (Android 13+ requires explicit grant for exact alarms). The app now
  falls back to inexact alarms automatically, and also catches up on missed emissions
  every time you open the app.
- New: tap the ⋮ menu (top right) → "Report Issue" to open a GitHub issue, or
  "Submit Log" to share a diagnostic log file.
- New: version number shown under the app title so you know which build you're on.
- New: if scheduled tasks still aren't working, a warning banner appears with a link
  to grant the Alarms & reminders permission.
